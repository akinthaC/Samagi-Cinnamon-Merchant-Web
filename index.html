About Us

Since 2017...

Samagi Cinnamon, your trusted partner in the world of premium spices. Established in the heart of Sri Lanka, in Katandola, we specialize in exporting the finest quality Ceylon cinnamon, pepper, cardamom, and a variety of other spices.

With a dedication to excellence, we ensure that every spice we export meets the highest standards of quality and flavor, sourced directly from local farmers and processed with care.

At Samagi Cinnamon, we value sustainability, ethical sourcing, and building long-term relationships with both our suppliers and clients. We believe in promoting fair trade practices and contributing to the well-being of our community while delivering products that satisfy the most discerning tastes.
